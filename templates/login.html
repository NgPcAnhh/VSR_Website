VIRTUAL SPEAKING ROOM

INTRODUCTION:

_____________________________________________________

In the virtual speaking room, users can experience the real exam of the IELTS speaking computer-based
test and strengthen their mentality and essential speaking skills to prepare for the real test.

Solution:

_____________________________________________________

It's a solution that saves time, money, effort, and manpower for the speaking practice phase, which normally requires an additional person to act as an examiner. Moreover, the app offers various modes such as full test practice or individual practice for part one or part two and three.

Advantage:

With an easy-to-use, user-friendly interface and nearly 90% of the processes automated and similar to the actual speaking test, it helps users become familiar with and practice so they won't feel unfamiliar during the official exam.

Notice:
Please note that the software does not have a grading function because AI CANNOT replace humans in grading speaking tests.
